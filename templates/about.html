About the Loan Eligibility Model

This page explains each input feature used in our machine learning model, along with visual insights to help you understand how the model works.

Loan Purpose and Public Records Analysis

These charts show how the purpose of the loan and public record count relate to loan repayment status. Most loans are taken for debt consolidation, followed by credit card purposes. Borrowers with fewer public records tend to have a better repayment history.

Income and Installment Distribution

This visualization displays the distribution of annual income and installment amounts among applicants. Income follows a normal-like distribution, while installment amounts are right-skewed ‚Äî meaning most applicants have moderate monthly payments.

Feature Correlation Heatmap

Model Performance ‚Äì Confusion Matrix

The confusion matrix summarizes model predictions. It shows how well the model differentiates between applicants who will repay (label 0) and those who won‚Äôt (label 1). The model correctly classifies most fully-paid cases but still has room to improve in predicting defaults.
